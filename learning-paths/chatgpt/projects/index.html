Projects

Use Projects to Stay Organized

Projects let you group chats around a theme, client, or use case. They act like folders, keeping your ideas cleanly separated.

Why use Projects?

  • Maintain context for different clients or teams
  • Save prompts, results, and refinements in one place
  • Reduce cross-contamination of ideas

How to Create a Project:

  1. Click the Projects tab in the sidebar (top left, next to Chats)
  2. Click New Project
  3. Give it a clear name (e.g. "Product Strategy", "Client A", or "AI Course Design")
  4. Start chatting — all conversations will be saved in that context

Tip: You can move existing chats into Projects too. Just drag and drop.
